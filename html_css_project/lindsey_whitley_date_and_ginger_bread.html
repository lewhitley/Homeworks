Date and Ginger Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cups chopped dried dates
  • 5 tablespoons butter
  • 1 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups self-rising flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons chopped crystallized ginger

Description

Date and Ginger Cake is a delicious and dense Australian cake. This recipe makes 2 loaves.

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Boil water and dates for 5 minutes. Add butter and sugar, stir, and boil for 3 more minutes. Let cool at least 10 minutes before moving on.
  3. While mixture cools, mix flour, baking soda, and ground ginger together.
  4. After date mixture cools, add eggs and cyrstallized ginger. Mix well, then add dry ingredients. Mix well.
  5. Pour into two standard size loaf pans that have been sprayed with Pam.
  6. Bake 35 to 40 minutes. Allow to cool completely before removing from pan.

Original recipe from the Country Women's Association cookbook with conversion to Imperial measurements by Laureen Whitley. CWA Recipe
